Leo Lu
Founder

Mr. Lu possesses 19+ years of technology management experience, with more than half at strategic and executive management levels. Leo has been employed by top organizations such as IBM, Franklin Templeton, DHL, and EarthLink. Leo was part of a pioneering SaaS company that raised $50 million-plus and disrupted the market landscape through the patented development of application virtualization and streaming technologies. During his tenure, he architected and led teams of engineers to package vendor software from Allscripts and Misys to add streaming as a service with Medical ISV’s, healthcare organizations, and large hospitals to be available in their Healthcare Interchange Exchange (HIE). The company went on to strike licensing deals with Microsoft, Citrix, and VMware. In his off-time, Leo is an active community leader with Rotary International, LA5. Phillip Hoang
Founder

Phillip is the software tools lead at Raytheon Company. Phillip is responsible for software methodology and tools to increase productivity through the software center of more than 900 software engineers. Software methodologies include: agile, continuous integration, static analysis, test driven design, and model driven design. His responsibilities also include research and development of new methodologies and tools. Phillip’s engineering roles include being a subject matter expert for video tracking, super-resolution image processing, and free and open source software (FOSS). He currently is chair of the FOSS technology interchange group. Outside of Raytheon, Phillip is a real estate investor and co-owner of Alva Dynamics LLC, an engineering and manufacturing company. He participates as founding President of WECEED, a non-profit organization focused on community, environment, education, and development. His previous leadership roles included being founding President of the National Association of Asian American Professionals (NAAAP) of Orange County, board member of the Vietnamese American Chamber of Commerce, Asian Business Association of Orange County, Raytheon Management Club, Raytheon Asian Pacific Association, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, Association of Computing Machinery, and Alpha Phi Omega. Phillip holds a B.S. in Computer Science, B.S. in Electrical Engineering, and a minor in Economics from the University of California, Riverside.
