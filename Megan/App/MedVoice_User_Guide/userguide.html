Health Awareness With Social Support

MedVoice strives to raise health awareness with social support using its ability to display possible health predispositions using voice recognition, your device's camera and care client inputs.

Care clients also have the ability to:

  • Participate in peer to peer chats,
  • View their health history,
  • Form a social network with care givers and other care clients,
  • View medical articles, journals and other medical media.
