Peer to Peer Chatting

MedVoice strives to raise health awareness with social support. Using voice recognition, your device's camera and care client inputs, MedVoice has the ability to display possible health predispositions. Care clients may also participate in peer to peer chats, view their health history, and form a social network with care givers and other care clients, and view scholarly articles, journals and other media.
